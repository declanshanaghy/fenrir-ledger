A minimal, flat vector-style icon mark inspired by a financial institution's iconic arc symbol. The design consists of a single bold red arc (hex #E11A27) that curves upward like an umbrella or bridge, with a short vertical navy-blue stroke (hex #003B70) rising from the center beneath it — evoking a stylized letter "t" crossbar viewed from a distance. The arc is a thick, smooth semicircular stroke (approximately 180 degrees but slightly less, roughly 150 degrees) with tapered ends, sitting above the vertical bar. No text, no wordmark, no gradients. Pure flat color on a transparent background. The icon uses only two colors: navy blue (#003B70) and red (#E11A27). Designed for extreme clarity at small sizes (16-24px): the arc is the dominant visual element taking up about 60% of the width, the vertical stroke is centered and about 15% of the width. Clean geometric shapes, uniform stroke weights, SVG-reproducible with just 2-3 path elements. Square aspect ratio. Minimal negative space. The overall impression is an abstract arc-over-pillar symbol that reads as a bold, confident financial mark.
