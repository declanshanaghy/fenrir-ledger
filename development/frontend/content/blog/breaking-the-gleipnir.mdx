          <div class="work-body">
            <p>No favicon existed. In Next.js App Router, placing <span class="mono">icon.svg</span> in <span class="mono">app/</span> causes the framework to inject a <span class="mono">&lt;link rel="icon"&gt;</span> tag automatically — modern browsers stop requesting <span class="mono">/favicon.ico</span>, ending the 404 entirely.</p>
            <p>The icon is a gold <span class="hl">ᚠ Fehu rune</span> on a void-black rounded square: a vertical stave from y=4 to y=28 at x=12, two right-branching diagonal strokes. Fehu is the first rune of FENRIR — fitting as the app's permanent mark. Stroke width 2.5 on the stave, 2 on the branches, all in <span class="mono">#c9920a</span>.</p>
          </div>

          <div class="norm-box">
            <p class="norm-label">Decision</p>
            <p class="norm-body">SVG favicon over ICO — SVG scales perfectly at any size, matches the dark Nordic design system, and lets Next.js handle injection automatically without a build step.</p>
          </div>
