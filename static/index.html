THE CHAINS THEY FORGED FOR YOU

Fáfnir's traps are not loud. They arrive like silk. They hold like iron. Three chains wait for every wolf.

ᚲ

The Fee-Serpent

The annual fee asks nothing when you open the card. It waits. One year later it strikes — while you sleep, while you spend, while you forget you forged the chain. By then, Fáfnir has already taken his tithe.

ᚺ

The Promo Tide

The welcome-mead flows for exactly one season. Earn the threshold spend or watch Hati swallow the moon. When the promo window closes, the gold that was yours returns to the dragon's hoard, unclaimed.

ᚠ

The Unclaimed Plunder

The opening plunder was yours. The sign-up bonus, the statement credits, the Skuld-set deadline — all faded unmet, untracked, unreached. The wolf who does not hunt goes hungry.

Fáfnir set these traps. Fenrir was bred to spring them.

ᛊ

WHAT THE WOLF WATCHES

Six gifts from the great wolf's ledger. Every chain tracked. Every deadline watched. Every reward that is yours — kept.

ᚲ

Sköll & Hati

Two wolves chase your deadlines. Sköll hunts the annual fee; Hati runs the promo moon. Watch them close. The moment they catch it, Ragnarök begins.

ᚺ

The Norns' Weave

Urd records what was. Verdandi shows what is. Skuld reveals what shall be — your open bonus windows, your promo cliffs, your spend thresholds. The Norns weave whether you watch or not.

ᛟ

The Ledger of Fates

Every debt-chain in your Pack, named and weighed. Reward balances, credit limits, fee schedules — the complete war-spoils of a churner who misses nothing.

ᛏ

Valhalla

Closed cards are not forgotten. They are honored. The Hall of the Honored Dead records every chain broken, every fee avoided, every dragon slain. A warrior counts their kills. So does the ledger.

ᛉ

The Howl

When chains tighten, Fenrir howls. Huginn and Muninn carry warnings before the fee-serpent strikes — days counted, thresholds flagged, windows closing. The wolf does not sleep when the chains move.

ᛗ

The Nine Realms

Each card lives in a realm — Asgard to Muspelheim, Vanaheim to Hel. The realm tells the story at a glance: earning, warning, burning, or honored dead. No badge more complex than the cosmos needs to be.

The wolf does not rush blindly. Three deliberate movements. Three runes carved. One chain broken.
