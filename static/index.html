THE CHAINS THEY FORGED FOR YOU

The traps aren't loud. They're quiet by design — dates buried in terms, thresholds easy to miss, windows that close without ceremony. Three of them catch most people.

ᚲ

The Fee-Serpent

The annual fee is zero work for the issuer and easy to forget for you. It charges automatically, on a date you set and then lost. No warning. No negotiation. It was always going to land — the question is whether you were ready.

ᚺ

The Promo Tide

Every sign-up offer has an expiration. Spend threshold by this date. Earn the bonus before the window closes. Miss it, and the points that were practically yours vanish — not stolen, just quietly allowed to expire. Hati runs. The moon doesn't wait.

ᚠ

The Unclaimed Plunder

Statement credits. Quarterly bonuses. Rewards that require activation. The issuers know most cardholders won't claim them — that's the business model. The wolf who tracks what's owed collects what others leave behind.

The issuers built these traps carefully. Fenrir was built to dismantle them.

ᛊ

WHAT THE WOLF WATCHES

Six tools that track every card in your household — deadlines, balances, open windows, and history. Nothing slips. Nothing closes unnoticed. Everything that's yours stays yours.

ᚲ

Sköll & Hati

Two deadline trackers named for the wolves who chase the sun and moon across the sky. Sköll watches your annual fee dates; Hati watches your promo windows. Each one counts down. When either catches its prey, the cost is real.

ᚺ

The Norns' Weave

Past, present, and future — what you've spent, where your balances stand today, and what needs to happen before each deadline closes. The Norns have always known the shape of fate. The ledger makes it readable.

ᛟ

The Ledger of Fates

Every card in your household, laid out cleanly. Annual fees, credit limits, reward balances, issuer details, fee schedules. The full picture — not scattered across apps and email threads, but in one place, always current.

ᛏ

Valhalla

Closed cards don't disappear. They're archived — every fee you avoided, every bonus you collected, every card you closed at exactly the right moment. Your record. Proof of a strategy that paid off.

ᛉ

The Howl

Alerts before deadlines, not after. The Howl fires when a fee date is approaching, when a promo window is tightening, when a spend threshold needs attention. You choose how much warning you want. The wolf doesn't sleep on deadline watch.

ᛗ

The Nine Realms

Each card carries a status badge drawn from the Nine Realms — a single word that tells you exactly where it stands. Earning, warning, burning, or closed with honor. A glance at the dashboard and you know the state of every chain.
