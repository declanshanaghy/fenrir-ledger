Safety Banner Variants

This wireframe documents all safety-related banner variants used across the Import Wizard. Each variant is shown with placement context and accessibility annotations.

Variant 1: Full Safety Banner

Placement: Method Selection step (Step 1), above the method cards. Always visible. Not dismissible.

⚠ Protect Your Secrets

Your spreadsheet should contain card names, issuers, fees, and dates — never full card numbers, CVVs, PINs, or passwords. Fenrir Ledger does not need or store sensitive payment credentials.

What to include

  • Card name / product (e.g., "Sapphire Preferred")
  • Issuer (e.g., "Chase", "Amex")
  • Annual fee amount
  • Open date
  • Bonus details (spend, deadline, status)
  • Notes

Never include

  • Full credit card numbers (16 digits)
  • CVV / security codes
  • PINs
  • Account passwords
  • Social Security numbers
  • Bank account numbers

ACCESSIBILITY: role="alert" for immediate screen reader announcement. Heading "Protect Your Secrets" is Norse-voiced but clear in meaning. Two-column layout collapses to single column below 480px. THEME: amber/gold border (#c9920a), subtle amber background ~8% opacity. "Never include" list items styled in a subdued warning tone (not red).

Variant 2: Compact Safety Reminder

Placement: Steps 2A, 2B, 2C (URL entry, Picker, CSV upload). Below the back link, above the main content. Not dismissible.

⚠ Never include card numbers, CVVs, PINs, or passwords in your file. Details

Single-line reminder with a "Details" link that expands to Variant 1 content or scrolls to it. ACCESSIBILITY: role="note" (less urgent than role="alert" since user already saw the full banner). "Details" link is a button that toggles an inline expansion of the full include/exclude lists. Touch target for "Details" meets 44px min-height.

Variant 3: Sensitive Data Warning (Preview Step)

Placement: Preview step (Step 4), at the top of the card list, only if the LLM sets sensitiveDataWarning: true.

⚠ Potential Sensitive Data Detected

Our extraction process detected data that may include credit card numbers or security codes. This data has been excluded from the import, but we recommend you review and remove it from your source spreadsheet.

  • No card numbers, CVVs, or PINs have been imported
  • The extracted cards below contain only safe fields
  • Review your spreadsheet and remove any sensitive columns

CONDITIONAL: Only rendered when the LLM response includes sensitiveDataWarning. ACCESSIBILITY: role="alert" since this is critical safety information the user must see. THEME: amber/gold border and background, matching Variant 1. This banner is NOT dismissible -- it stays visible throughout the preview step.

Variant 4: Post-Share Reminder (Success Step, Path A only)

Placement: Success step (Step 6), below the success message, only for Path A (Share URL) imports.

🔒
You can now remove the public share from your spreadsheet. Fenrir has inscribed the data in the ledger. Your spreadsheet no longer needs to be publicly accessible.

CONDITIONAL: Only shown after a successful Path A import. ACCESSIBILITY: role="note" -- informational, not urgent. THEME: neutral border, subtle background. Lock icon suggests security. This is dismissible (user can close it or it disappears when the modal closes).
