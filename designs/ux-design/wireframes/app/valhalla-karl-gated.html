Mobile rules
  • Two tab bars: bottom nav (route tabs) + dashboard content tabs (Active / All / Valhalla).
  • Both show Karl lock indicator on Valhalla. Both trigger the same KarlUpsellDialog.
  • Dialog bottom-sheet: anchored to bottom, full-width, not floating.
  • Background content dims to ~20% opacity when dialog is open.
  • Bottom nav also dims behind open dialog.
  • Touch target for "K" badge: badge is visual only — entire tab item is the touch target (56px tall × ~94px wide).
  • iOS: padding-bottom: env(safe-area-inset-bottom) on bottom nav.
