User Menu — Theme Position Reorder

Issue #1483 — Move Theme to 2nd-last position (before Sign out) in the user dropdown

Component: ProfileDropdown in LedgerTopBar.tsx — affects desktop and mobile viewports

A — Current Order vs Expected Order

Theme is currently 2nd (immediately after My Cards), giving it false prominence for an infrequent preference. It should move to 2nd-last (before Sign out), grouped with lower-frequency utility actions.

Current Order — WRONG

ProfileDropdown (current)
1 My Cards
2 Theme ← WRONG (too prominent)
3 Account
4 Household
5 Settings
6 Sign out

Theme sits at position 2, immediately after My Cards. This elevates a low-frequency preference above navigation items (Account, Household, Settings) that users access far more often.

Expected Order — CORRECT

ProfileDropdown (expected)
1 My Cards
2 Account
3 Household
4 Settings
5 Theme ← 2nd-last (CORRECT)
6 Sign out

Theme sits at position 5 (2nd-last), just above Sign out. Both are infrequent-use or destructive-ish actions that belong at the bottom of the menu. Navigation destinations (Account, Household, Settings) now occupy the more prominent mid-section.
