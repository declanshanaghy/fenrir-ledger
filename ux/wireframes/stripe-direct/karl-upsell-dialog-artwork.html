Component tree (left column, top section)

    <div aria-hidden shimmer-overlay /> ← gold shimmer on hover (same as /features)

      src="/images/features/{featureImage}-dark.png"

    />
    <Image
      src="/images/features/{featureImage}-light.png"

</div>
