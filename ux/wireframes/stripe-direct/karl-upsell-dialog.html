Mobile rules
  • Dialog anchored to bottom of screen (flex align-items: flex-end).
  • Width: 100% of frame — full-bleed bottom sheet, not a floating card.
  • featureTagline hidden on mobile — saves critical vertical space.
  • Icon shrinks from 72px → 56px. Lock badge shrinks proportionally.
  • All buttons maintain 44px min touch target.
  • CTA remains full-width — easiest tap target on small screen.
  • "Not now" remains visible below CTA — no hidden dismiss path on mobile.
  • Backdrop click above sheet still dismisses (same as desktop).
